About

Modder, first and foremost.

I've been building game mods for 6+ years, starting with texture swaps and working up to full character imports, custom fighters, and moveset design across a dozen engines.

I work across Smash Ultimate, Brawlhalla, Risk of Rain 2, Borderlands, Skyrim, Fallout, Resident Evil, Guilty Gear Strive, Sonic, Palworld, and Marvel Rivals — and I'll scope a quote for engines outside that list too.

My approach is simple: a fixed scope before anything starts, honest timelines, and a finished mod that installs cleanly and plays the way it looked in the pitch. No half-finished files, no disappearing after payment.
